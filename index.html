Derivative

Power Rule:

3.1 Derivative Definition

Derivative of `f(x)`
`f'(x) = lim_(h->0) (f(x+h) - f(x))/h`
Alternate Derivative Definition
`f'(x) = lim_(x->a) (f(x) - f(a))/(x-a)`
Where `x` and `f(x)` are generic and `a` and `f(a)` are known
Graph of `f(x)` vs `f'(x)`
`f(x) = x^3-3x`

`f'(x) = 3x^2-3`

3.2 Differentiability

For a `f(x)` to be differentiable at an `x`. `f(x)` has to be continous and locally linear at that `x`

Graphs where f' DNE
1. Corner: `y=|x|`

2. Cusp: `y=x^(2/3)`

Vertical Tangent: `y=`x3
Symmetric Diff. Quotient
`f' = (f(a+h) - f(a-h))/(2h)`
graphing calculator uses `h=0.001`
Example Problem
`f(x) = x^2 - 1 , x <= 1`
    ` 2x - 1, x>1`
`f' = lim_(h->0^-) ((1+h)^2 - 0)/(h)` `= lim_(h->0^+) (2(1+h) - 0)/(h) `
`f' = lim_(h->0^-) (2h+h^2)/(h)` `= lim_(h->0^+) (1+2h)/(h) `
`f' = 2 = +∞`
`f' = DNE`

3.3 Rules of Differentiation

Rules
Power Rule: `(d/dx)(x^n)=nx^(n-1)`
Constant(c): `(d/dx)(c)=0`
Const x Plynml: `(d/dx)(c*u)=c*u'`
Addition: `(d/dx)(u+-v)=u'+-v'`
Multiplication: `(d/dx)(u*v)=u*v'+v*u'`
Division: `(d/dx)(u/v)=(u*v'-v*u')/v^2`
Second Derivative
`d/(dx)f(x) = f'(x)`
`d/(dx)f'(x) = f''(x)`
`f''(x)=(d^2y)/(dx^2)`
